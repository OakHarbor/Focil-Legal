For those fleeing persecution, we offer compassionate representation in asylum and refugee cases. We assist skilled workers, investors, and businesses with employment-based immigration matters. From H-1B and L-1 visa applications to permanent residency through employment, we provide strategic guidance for work authorization and green card processing. The path to U.S. citizenship represents the culmination of your immigration journey. We guide clients through naturalization applications, citizenship test preparation, and oath ceremony processes. ### Immigration Appeals and Federal Litigation

When cases require appellate review or federal court intervention, our team provides skilled representation before the Board of Immigration Appeals and federal circuit courts. We handle complex immigration appeals, mandamus actions, and federal litigation with the expertise that comes from years of specialized practice.
